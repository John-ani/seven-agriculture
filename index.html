About Seven Agriculture

Seven Agriculture is dedicated to advancing precision farming and organic agriculture through technology and sustainability. Headquartered in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, we utilize AI, automation, and data-driven solutions to optimize farming efficiency.

Our mission is to enhance food security, improve yield management, and promote sustainable farming for a healthier future.
